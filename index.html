The NFy Project

NFy is an attempt to make a fast and lightweight application for playing .ogg and .wav files. With MP3 Support coming soon.


NFy has multiple projects, all open source, closed source, and abandoned (WIPs)
And Languages used are C++, C#, GDScript, Rust, and Python.
